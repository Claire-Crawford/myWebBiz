a bit about me

I'm Claire Crawford - FREESPIRITED DESIGNER AND SELF PROCLAIMED TECH-NERD. I craft unique, intuitive websites to empower women and help to bring their small-business dream to fruition.

I was somewhat of a late bloomer when it came to finding my calling. For many years my focus was on raising my children. I also ran a small, home-based business selling my art. It was during this time that I created my first website - not so much out of want than out of necessity (I commissioned a web designer on two occasions, with disappointing results). Thus began my love affair with web development. The pairing of artistic design with the challenging technical aspects of coding a webpage seemed to tick off all the major boxes for "dream job". I was still busy raising children so building a new career seemed an unrealistic goal.

Fast forward several years. Now, with children grown, I made the decision to go back to college. I'd rather eat a dirt sandwich than do that again (I'm laughing but, seriously, NO. I love to learn but too introverted for the college environment). However, I learned a ton, earned a degree in Computer Information/Web Development and even pulled off a 4.0 GPA ... #personalgoal

I've never been more certain that I'm doing what I'm meant to do. Crafting impactful websites for enterprising women isn't just outrageously enjoyable for me; It fulfills my heart's longing for a career that supports and empowers the dreams of others. What could be better than that, right?

You've worked tirelessly, pouring your and soul into your business and craft. You're embracing vulnerability and bravely putting yourself out there to the world. In case you didn't know - that is serious BADASSARY! As a professional web designer/developer, I have the joy of partnering with you on this part of your journey (so exciting!!). Together, we will build a website that mirrors your vision, celebrates your passion and ultimately connects you with an audience that grows your business and - let's keep it real here - makes you money!

I've been where you are and I know this whole process can seem daunting. Relax. We got this.

Claire
